# PlaySoundDieAccessory

## Why it was written

To let things (Leggies, mainly) play a sound on death.
___

## What it does

It has a SerializeField array of audio clips, on death, it uses [[LeggiesLibrary]], LeggiesSounds' PlayRandomSoundFrom to play one at random.
___

## How to use it

Attach to what would make a sound on death and assign a pool of death sounds.
