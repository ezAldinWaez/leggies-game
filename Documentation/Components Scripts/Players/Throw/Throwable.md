# Throwable

## Why it was written

To make an object throwable.
___

## What it does

It manages the throwable object; its shaking, sounding, properties, etc. [[Throw]] is what actually throws.
___

## How to use it

Attach it to a prefab that you will use as a throwable object. Just that; there is nothing to configure. Well, you have to put it as a property of [[Throw]] in the players.
